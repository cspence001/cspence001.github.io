Max Temperature

For this chart, the temperature of each city in the dataset was taken and rendered against it's established latitude point. From the model, we are able to determine that the temperature is inversely related to latitude. As latitude increases, the temperature decreases. Therefore, as we get closer to the equator, at 0 degrees latitude, we experience increasingly warmer temperatures.
